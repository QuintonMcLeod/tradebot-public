## What It Actually Does

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/creator.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CREATOR</b>:<br>"When you click that shiny button, here's what happens while you're sitting there staring. And I'm going step by step because I want you to understand exactly how much work this thing is doing that you were too lazy to do yourself:"</td></tr></table>

1. **Reads your symbols** — Looks at every ticker in the profile (EUR_USD, BTCUSD, XAUUSD, etc.) and figures out *what* you're trading: forex, crypto, metals, stocks, or some chaos mix of all four.

2. **Analyzes market characteristics** — Different asset classes need different approaches. Forex has sessions and spreads. Crypto has 24/7 volatility. Metals are slow movers with sudden spikes. The AI knows this because it read the manual. Unlike you.

3. **Picks your strategies** — Assigns the best strategy *per asset class*, not just one blanket strategy. Your crypto gets a crypto-optimized strategy. Your forex gets the Conductor. Your gold doesn't get a scalper because gold doesn't scalp. Gold does what gold does — sit there and occasionally surge like it remembered it has somewhere to be.

4. **Tunes your safety shields** — ATR Armor, Drawdown Breaker, Session Lockout, Leverage Sentry — there are 15+ safety guards. The AI enables the right ones and sets appropriate thresholds. Conservative for forex, looser for crypto (because crypto needs room to breathe or it'll get stopped out every 3 minutes).

5. **Configures performance modes** — Trailing stops, pyramiding depth, profit targets. The AI picks modes that match your asset mix.

6. **Sets up trend detection** — Which trend indicators should be active? EMA crossovers? MACD? ADX? The AI enables the right combination for your specific markets.

7. **Saves everything** — All changes go directly into your config. No clipboard. No copy-paste. No "remember to also change that other thing." It just works.

## What Gets Configured

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/professor.png" width="150"></td><td><b>PROFESSOR</b>:<br>"The AI doesn't just pick a strategy and call it a day. It configures <b>six entire categories</b> of settings. Let me enumerate them because I am, at my core, a man who loves a good list:"</td></tr></table>

### 🎯 Strategy Selection
Which strategy runs on which asset class. The AI considers your specific symbols — EUR/USD? It knows that needs a session-aware forex strategy. BTCUSD? It knows crypto needs 24/7 coverage. Mixed portfolio? Each asset class gets its own strategy.

### ⚙️ Strategy Workshop
The tuning knobs *within* the strategy. Risk per trade percentage. Risk-reward ratio. Pyramiding depth and risk scaling. Hold time limits. These are the numbers most people either leave at defaults (bad) or randomly change until something breaks (worse).

### 🛡️ Safety & Shields
The full defensive stack. Which safety guards are on, which are off, and what their trigger thresholds are.

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/chad.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CHAD</b>:<br>"The AI turned on 11 safety shields for my crypto profile. I counted. I was using three. THREE. I was basically trading in a bathrobe on a highway. Barefoot. At night."</td></tr></table>

### 📈 Performance & Profits
Trailing stops, breakeven trails, performance modes (Sniper, Regime Sync, House Money, etc.). These are the "how do I keep my profits" settings that separate accounts that grow from accounts that give everything back by Friday.

### 📊 Trend Detection
Which trend indicators to activate. Heavier detection for forex (which trends in waves), lighter or different indicators for crypto (which trends like a drunk person on a skateboard).

### 💰 Risk Parameters
Position sizing, max exposure, daily loss limits. The stuff that determines whether a bad day costs you 2% or 40%.

## Pro Tips

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/professor.png" width="150"></td><td><b>PROFESSOR</b>:<br>"Five rules for using AI Optimize without embarrassing yourself:"</td></tr></table>

1. **Make a test profile first** — Duplicate your current profile, run AI Optimize on the copy, and compare. Don't experiment on your live profile at 2 AM. Nothing good happens to configs at 2 AM.

2. **Read the reasoning** — The history panel doesn't just show what changed, it shows *why*. The AI's reasoning is often educational. You'll learn things about your own strategy you didn't know.

3. **Don't fight the AI** — If it turns something off that you had on, there's usually a reason. Check the reasoning before reflexively turning it back on. The AI read the manual. Did you?

4. **Re-run periodically** — Markets change. Your symbol list changes. What was optimal 3 months ago might not be optimal now.

5. **Compare profiles** — Run AI Optimize on your crypto and forex profiles separately. Notice how the configurations are completely different. That's the whole point — one size does NOT fit all.
