### How It Works (The Short Version)

The Autopilot reads a massive payload of JSON data every single cycle. It looks at your win rate. It looks at your recent trades. It looks at the safety gates that are currently blocking you. 

Then, it makes **decisions**. 

*   **Market is ranging?** It switches to Mean Reversion and lowers the take-profit target so you aren't waiting for a home run in a bunt game.
*   **Market is trending hard?** It switches to Trend Rider and widens the net.
*   **News is wild?** It activates the AI Sentiment Shield and tells you to go take a nap because the market is drunk.

### What Autopilot Specifically Prevents

* **Revenge Trading:** Oh, you took a loss? And now you want to double your lot size to 'make it back'? Autopilot will literal block the entry, lower your risk percentage, and tell you to go touch grass.
* **Fighting the Trend:** You think you're Michael Burry and you want to short the top of a raging bull market? Autopilot checks the H4 structure, sees it's bullish, and denies your request. Sit down.
* **News Gambling:** CPI drops in 5 minutes? Autopilot flips the Sentiment Shield on and watches you pout while the market chops 50 pips in both directions and liquidates everyone but you.
