## Three Good Days a Month

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/creator.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CREATOR</b>:<br>"This is the part where people's eyes widen. Where the math goes from 'interesting' to 'wait... what?' So pay attention."</td></tr></table>

You have a **$50,000 account** with **4% risk per trade**. That means every position risks **$2,000**. With a **2:1 reward-to-risk ratio**, every winner pays **$4,000**.

A good trading day doesn't mean every trade wins. It means the winners outpace the losers. On a solid day — say 2 clean winners and 1 loser — your net is:

> **(2 × $4,000) − (1 × $2,000) = $6,000 net profit**
