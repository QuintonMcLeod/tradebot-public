## Trap 1: FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/rookie.png" width="150"></td><td><b>ROOKIE</b>:<br>"Bitcoin jumped 5% while I was in the shower! Should I buy?!"</td></tr></table>

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/bear.png" width="150"></td><td><b>BEAR</b>:<br>"You want to buy at the TOP? Classic FOMO."</td></tr></table>

**Why It Happens:** Your brain treats missed profits like actual losses. Watching something go up without you hurts the same way losing money does.

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/bot.png" width="150"></td><td><b>THE BOT</b>:<br><em>"I don't watch Twitter. I don't read headlines. I don't know BTC jumped 5%. I evaluate structure. A 5% spike with no pullback fails the ICC test. No Correction → no trade."</em></td></tr></table>
