## Trade By SCI Alignment (The Mechanics)

Trade By SCI is about structure-first patience, not constant action. This bot is built to:

- **Wait for the model** (HTF alignment → correction/sweep → continuation) instead of early reaction entries. It doesn't jump on every green candle like a golden retriever chasing a tennis ball.
- **Say "no" loudly and clearly** via gates + reason codes (`NO_SWEEP`, `NO_CONTINUATION`), so discipline is enforced without guesswork. No trade is better than a bad trade. Always.
- **Stay tradable in real venues** by enforcing constraints (no fake shorts on long-only spot). Because paper profits don't pay rent.
- **Be auditable** so execution is repeatable. Every "no trade" is logged with a reason. No mystery. No "the bot just... didn't do anything." There's always a reason.

2.  **"Stand aside" is the default**: It will not manufacture trades. If nothing looks good, it does nothing. Which is the single hardest thing for humans to do — absolutely nothing — and the bot does it effortlessly.
3.  **Selection vs Readiness**: Separates "Which chart to watch?" (Selection) from "Is it time?" (Readiness). Because knowing where to look and knowing when to act are two very different skills.
4.  **Deterministic Gates**: The AI suggests, but the Code enforces. Hard gates (Range, Volume, Structure) must pass. The AI can have opinions. The code has standards.
5.  **Commitment Mode**: Once in, it manages. It does not re-guess every candle. It doesn't second-guess itself every 15 minutes like you do with every text you've ever sent.
