## What the Hell Is Context Masking?

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/creator.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CREATOR</b>:<br>"Okay, listen to me closely. You know how you act different when you're at work versus when you're at a bar with your friends? At work, you're 'Professional Greg'. At the bar, you're 'Tequila Greg'. ## How It Actually Works (No Nerd Talk, I Promise)

| Step | What Happens | The Analogy |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **1. The Blueprint** | The bot grabs your Global Profile — the master settings. | You wake up in your default outfit: white tee, jeans, sneakers. Basic. Functional. Nobody's impressed. |
| **2. The Fitting Room** | Right before a strategy trades, the bot pulls it aside into a private dressing room. | "Hey RoboCop, come here for a second. We need to talk about your outfit." |
| **3. The Mask** | The bot overlays the strategy's *personal* settings on top of the global ones. Only the things you specifically changed get swapped out.
