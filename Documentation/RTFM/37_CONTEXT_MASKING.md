## What the Hell Is Context Masking?

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/creator.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CREATOR</b>:<br>"Alright, let me explain this in a way that even my aunt — who thinks Wi-Fi is 'the internet cloud' — could understand.<br><br>You know how you act different depending on who you're with? You're one person at work. You're a different person with your boys on a Friday night. You're a <em>completely</em> different person when your mom calls and asks why you haven't visited in three weeks. Same person, different <b>mask</b>.<br><br>That's what Context Masking is. Each strategy gets to put on its <em>own</em> mask before it goes out to trade.<br><br>RoboCop wants a 3R target and tight stops? Fine. That's his mask. Rubberband Reaper wants wide Bollinger Bands and a conservative 2R target? Fine. That's <em>his</em> mask. They go out, they do their thing, they come back, they take the mask off. The original profile — the 'real you' — is still sitting at home in boxers watching Netflix. Completely untouched."</td></tr></table>

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/chad.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CHAD</b>:<br>"So it's like... identity fraud but for trading strategies?"</td></tr></table>

## How It Actually Works (No Nerd Talk, I Promise)

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/creator.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CREATOR</b>:<br>"Here's the play-by-play. Four steps. Even my barber could follow this and he still can't figure out Venmo."</td></tr></table>

| Step | What Happens | The Analogy |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **1. The Blueprint** | The bot grabs your Global Profile — the master settings. | You wake up in your default outfit: white tee, jeans, sneakers. Basic. Functional. Nobody's impressed. |
| **2. The Fitting Room** | Right before a strategy trades, the bot pulls it aside into a private dressing room. | "Hey RoboCop, come here for a second. We need to talk about your outfit." |
| **3. The Mask** | The bot overlays the strategy's *personal* settings on top of the global ones. Only the things you specifically changed get swapped out. Everything else stays the same. | "You're keeping the jeans. But we're swapping the white tee for a leather jacket and giving you a 3R target instead of 2R. Now go out there and handle your business." |
| **4. The Untouched Original** | After the trade, the Global Profile is still sitting there untouched. Clean. Virgin. Like nothing happened. | The white tee is still in the drawer. It doesn't even know RoboCop went out. It didn't get a text. It's fine. |
