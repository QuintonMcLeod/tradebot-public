## So What Should I Actually Expect?

<table><tr><td width="170"><img src="img/creator.png" width="150"></td><td><b>CREATOR</b>:<br>"Here's the honest answer. Frame it. Put it on your wall. Tattoo it on your forearm. Whatever it takes."</td></tr></table>

- **Week 1:** The bot is calibrating. Treat this week like orientation at a new job. Nobody produces results in orientation. You're filling out paperwork and figuring out where the bathroom is.
  
- **Month 1:** You'll see trades — wins and losses. The equity curve will wiggle. Don't touch anything. Don't panic. Don't call anyone. Don't uninstall the bot and reinstall it. Just... breathe.

- **Month 3:** You should have enough data to see a pattern. Is the overall trend going up? Even slightly? Even on a squint? Good. That's progress.

- **Month 6:** The compounding effect should be visible on your equity chart. Not dramatic. Not life-changing. But *there*. A gentle slope upward. Like a hiking trail, not a rocket launch.

- **Year 1:** This is where it starts to matter. If you haven't touched anything, haven't panic-closed, haven't "optimized" the settings every 3 days based on one bad trade, and haven't done the financial equivalent of pulling a plant out of the soil to check if the roots are growing — you should be meaningfully ahead of where you started.
